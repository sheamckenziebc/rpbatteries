Professional Custom Battery Pack Services

High-quality, custom lithium-ion batteries engineered to your exact specifications with premium cells and safety-first construction

E-Bike & E-Scooter Battery Packs

Custom battery packs designed for electric bicycles and scooters. Any voltage (24V, 36V, 48V, 52V, 60V+), any capacity, with high-current discharge capabilities. Built with premium cells from LG, Molicel, Samsung, and EVE. Includes smart BMS with balancing, overcurrent protection, and optional Bluetooth monitoring. Custom enclosures available to fit tight spaces or unique mounting requirements.

esk8 (Electric Skateboard) Battery Systems

High-performance battery packs engineered for electric skateboard enthusiasts. Tabless cells and copper bus bars available for extreme current applications. Flexible form factors to fit various deck configurations. Built for high discharge rates with excellent thermal management. Premium safety features including individual cell monitoring and temperature protection.

E-Foil & Hydrofoil Battery Systems

Waterproof battery solutions for electric hydrofoils and surfboards. Marine-grade enclosures with proper sealing and corrosion resistance. High energy density for extended ride times. Advanced BMS with wireless monitoring capabilities. Custom shapes to integrate seamlessly with your board design while maintaining optimal weight distribution.

Custom Project Battery Solutions

Bespoke battery packs for unique applications and projects. We work with your specifications to create the perfect power solution. From robotics to portable power stations, solar storage to specialty vehicles. Comprehensive consultation to optimize cell choice, configuration, and safety systems for your specific requirements.

Smart BMS & Monitoring Systems

Advanced Battery Management Systems with Bluetooth connectivity for real-time monitoring. Track voltage, current, temperature, and individual cell status through smartphone apps. Programmable protection parameters, cell balancing, and comprehensive safety features. Optional GPS integration for theft protection and location tracking.

Custom Enclosures & 3D Printing

Professional enclosure design and manufacturing using high-temperature, durable polymers. Custom 3D-printed solutions for unique form factors and tight space constraints. OEM-style enclosures with proper ventilation, mounting points, and connector integration. Weather-resistant options with IP rating certifications available.
